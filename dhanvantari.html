My Hobby Story

Dhanvantari loves exploring new places and discovering different cultures through travel. During every trip, he enjoys tasting local foods and learning unique cooking styles. One summer, he traveled to a beautiful hill station with his family. Inspired by the delicious dishes he tasted there, he returned home and recreated them in his kitchen. His family loved the meals and encouraged him to keep practicing. Since then, traveling and cooking have become more than hobbies—they help him learn new skills, create happy memories, and connect with people from different backgrounds while enjoying exciting adventures and wonderful flavors.
